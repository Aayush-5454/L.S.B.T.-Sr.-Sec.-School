Welcome Note

Dear Students, Parents, and Staff,

I warmly welcome you to our thriving school community. At Sunrise International School, we are committed to nurturing not only intellectual growth but also emotional and ethical development. We believe that true education inspires curiosity, builds character, and prepares students for a changing world.

With a talented faculty and a future-focused curriculum, we create an environment that encourages collaboration, creativity, and confidence. Together, let us shape a generation that is compassionate, resilient, and capable of making a difference.

Dr. Asha Saini

Principal, L.S.B.T. Sr. Sec. School

Education is not just about learning facts but about developing critical thinking, compassion, and resilience. My commitment is to guide each student toward their full potential in a safe, supportive, and enriching environment.
