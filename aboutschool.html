About L.S.B.T. Sr. Sec. School

Welcome

Education is the single largest factor that shapes the head, heart, and mind, thereby developing an all-round personality. Education makes people easy to lead but difficult to drive: easy to govern, but impossible to enslave. Excellence is never granted but it is the reward of labor. L.S.B.T. also believes that quality is never an accident but it is always the result of high intentions, sincere efforts, intelligent directions, and skillful executions. It is distinct and progressive where quality education is imparted in an environment which is physically beneficial, academically sound and administratively independent.

Our Mission

The mission of the school is to provide an environment that lays emphasis on intellectual and emotional growth in which every student discovers and realizes his/her full potential and highest academic standards are achieved; where emphasis is laid on integrity, ethics, moral courage, hard work, responsibility and self discipline. L.S.B.T. prepares students to understand, contribute and be succeed in a rapidly changing society. We ensure that our students develop both the skills and competencies essential for success and leadership in the emerging creative economy. We also lead to generate practical and theoretical knowledge that enables people to understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to develop well rounded confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential. We at L.S.B.T., empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school: respect tolerance & inclusion and excellence. Our vision aligns with shifts in the Global Economy, Society and Environment which animates our mission and values like Social Engagement, Creativity, Innovation and Principles. It is required for individuals to deal with complex problems, pursue flexible career pathways and create change collaboratively.
